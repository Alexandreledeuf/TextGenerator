Why the heart beats..? ,
Because
It tries to escape
From man, a sinner,
And would achieve one day;
That is the day..!

Relieved from sinful
Thoughts, And ends
Committing immoral,
And reaches ‘haven or hell’,

When the heart wins;

When it happens...
