she looks like
she just came
off a three day binge
or
some nice cruise
never bothering
to wash the
ocean away.
that dazed
but satisfied look
pleased with all
that crosses her path
she does not take part
in my sorts of abuse.
she will share the space
with devils and spirits,
but kindly refuse.
a simple girl with
outrageous beauty.
what is it that she knows?
where is that spot
deep inside?
that spot where she
stashes strength?
does it even matter
with lips like that?
