心胆相照
战地英雄胆
平时志愿心
几人堪论剑
一曲觅知音
< br>
Be In Perfect Sympathy With Each Other
Usually willing heart
Heroic courage in the battle land
A few people can talk about swords
A piece of music seeks the bosom friends
