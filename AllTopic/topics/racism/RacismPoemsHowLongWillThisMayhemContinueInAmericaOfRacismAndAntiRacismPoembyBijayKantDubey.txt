How long will the mayhem continue to,
Rioting and chaos,
Anti-racism protests
Aligned with arson, loot and setting on fire,
Breaking and damaging of property,
How long,
How, long will this mayhem
In the name of protests,
How long,
How long this vandalism?
How long, how long will they,
The activists,
Anarchists and bohemian artists,
Leftists and so called rightists?
If all are wrong, are they right?
