The Free Verse
(Two Pairs of Couplets●Seven Words of Quatrain)
The landscape painting, moon lies at anchor in the lake light
The free verse, clouds stay mountains and plains
In the pond, water warm and lotus in early bloom
In the dream, feel sad, sober up late
2017/6/8/罗志海写诗翻译
