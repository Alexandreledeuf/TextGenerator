In Spring They Come Says Sapphic Stanza Poetry
- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
After disappearing winter appears spring
Blossom tulip, daffodil, snowdrop and rose,
Rhythmic airy music, birds sing go daylong
Color universe.
