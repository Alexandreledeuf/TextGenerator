Anacreontic
Verse it is
On the universe,
The vast nature
Full of the
Innumerable
Creatures and
Objects and
The whole creations,
Among them
The relations,
All these show
Love and care,
Depending on
One another,
And helping
One another;
Human should
Learn these all
And practise
In practical life,
To avoid all
The chaoses
In the society
Arise in time
And out of time.
