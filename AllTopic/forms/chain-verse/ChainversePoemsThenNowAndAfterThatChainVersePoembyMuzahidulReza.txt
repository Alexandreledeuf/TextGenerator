Then Now And After That (Chain Verse)
- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
Dear, forget your sweet past!
You were, you had;
Sense the poisonous present
You are, you have;
And get ready for worsened future just
You will be, you will have.
