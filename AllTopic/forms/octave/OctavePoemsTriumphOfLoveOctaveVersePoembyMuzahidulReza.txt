Triumph Of Love In Octave Verse:
- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
Open the door,
Hey! Loveless world of fear,
I am love
I have come, enough;
I will crush all the curse
All the hatred I will brush,
I will bless everyone
Shall be deprived no one.
