★ Rhyme of Quatrain（Two Pairs of Couplets）
☆ Quatrain & Translation by Luo Zhihai (1954-, China)
Frost spread wilderness and chrysanthemum pretty
Fog enveloped remote mountains and autumn mood thick
A bunch of heart fragrance floated both banks at this time
Rhyme of quatrain recalled this life
