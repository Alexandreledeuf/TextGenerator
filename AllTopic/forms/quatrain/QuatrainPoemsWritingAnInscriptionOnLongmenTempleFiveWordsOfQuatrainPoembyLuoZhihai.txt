★ Writing an Inscription on Longmen Temple (Five Words of Quatrain)
☆ Poetry by Lu Hai (? -? , Tang Dynasty, China)
☆ Translation by Luo Zhihai (1954-, China)
Window light in the forest mist
Heard jade bell sound in the water
Took part in Longhua Meeting
Stove smoke filled the evening wind
