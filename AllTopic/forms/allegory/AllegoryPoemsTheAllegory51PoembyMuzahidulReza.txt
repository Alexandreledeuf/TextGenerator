The Allegory - 51
- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
A teethless old boar
Neither can hunt nor is obedient to other
Rotten flesh eater
Lies under the tail of viper
As its flatterer
So to others a serious exterminator
On nasty life
Ugly staff
Is satisfied
To the open, dangerous spirit.
