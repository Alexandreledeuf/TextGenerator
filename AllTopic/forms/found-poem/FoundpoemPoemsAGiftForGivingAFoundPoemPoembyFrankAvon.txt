Forgiveness is a gift
we give ourselves; not
the person we forgive
but ourselves. When we harbor
resentment and vitriol
we only harm ourselves.
Everything is forgivable;
some things are inexcusable
but everything is forgivable.
*****
Note: A found poem based on 'How Living with and Loving Bruce Jenner Changed My Life Forever, ' by Linda Thompson.  (See Story for www citation.)
