Beauties with good ceremonies
Like chastity, honesty, equity;
Good ceremonies with beauties
With the muse of purity,
And all other good qualities
So, to look for beauties,
Need to go to good qualities
Beauties are always by good qualities;
So good qualities for beauty seekers
Make them good qualities gainers.
