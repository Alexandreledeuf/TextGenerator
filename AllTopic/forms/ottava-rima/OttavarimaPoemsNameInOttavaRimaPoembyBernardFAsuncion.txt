N-ame in Ottava Rima form
I-s also in Acrostic style;
C-ombination is truly warm,
O-pening the eyes to smile.
L-ovely lines make no harm,
L-etters are not obnoxious or vile;
A-nother word that brings euphoria
I-s the name in Ottava Rima.
Topic: Birthday of Nicollai Jaca (April 10)
Form: Acrostic Ottava Rima
