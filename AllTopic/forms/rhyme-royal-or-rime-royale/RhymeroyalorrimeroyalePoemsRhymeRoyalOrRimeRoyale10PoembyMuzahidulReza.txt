(Rhyme Royal Or Rime Royale - 10)
National flag, sign of independence
To achieve it we all fight hard for long,
Then sing for patriotism under which
We must hold it being very strong
Loving country, democracy, nation
Avoiding pretendence, hypocrisy
Settling all the noises that cry ugly.
