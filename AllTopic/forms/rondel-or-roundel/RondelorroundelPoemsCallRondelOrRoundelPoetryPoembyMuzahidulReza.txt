Call
Rondel Or Roundel Poetry
January 1, 2020
How many calls do you know?
I know a significant one
It is the call for prayer
It is call for good as truth;
There is a call for game
There is a call for damn,
How many calls do you know?
I know a significant one;
Call for taking anything
Call for donating anything
Call for utilizing something
How many calls do you know?
I know a significant one.
