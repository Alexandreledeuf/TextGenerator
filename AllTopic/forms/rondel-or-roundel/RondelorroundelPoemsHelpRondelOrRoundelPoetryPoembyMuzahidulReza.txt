Help
Rondel Or Roundel Poetry
January 1, 2020
Help in good is always good
Help in bad is always bad,
In both one does another helps
Methinks both do and help.
Help well the disabled ones
Again, help the unable ones.
Help in good is always good
Help in bad is always bad.
Help your relatives, friends
Help in good whole mankind,
Help the creatures living good,
Help in good is always good
Help in bad is always bad,
